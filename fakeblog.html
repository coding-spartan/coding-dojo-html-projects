Amelia Boone

World's Toughest Athlete!

Amelia Boone (Born September 27, 1983) is an American obstacle racer.

She used to be an attorney for the law firm of Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom.

She's currently employed by Apple Inc. in San Jose, CA.

As one of the most accomplished OCR athletes in the history of the sport, she has won multiple races (see below).

She is sponsored by Reebok, BeetElite, RockTape and has been featured in a Tough Mudder commercial.
